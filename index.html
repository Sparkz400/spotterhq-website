Live Tracking

See Every Aircraft in the Sky

Real-time tracking powered by community-driven ADS-B data. No registration or API keys required.

🛰

Live Aircraft Map

Real-time positions on cached OpenStreetMap tiles with 10-second auto-refresh. Color-coded markers distinguish military, civilian, and emergency squawk aircraft at a glance.

📸

AR Sky View

Point your camera at the sky and see aircraft overlaid in augmented reality. Uses compass and motion sensors with adjustable field-of-view — track up to 15 targets at once.

🎯

CPA Tracking

Lock onto any aircraft for dedicated Closest Point of Approach tracking. Live countdown, closing speed, bearing, and persistent notifications so you never miss the shot.

🛰

Satellites & Gliders

Extend beyond ADS-B with CelesTrak satellite orbital tracking and Open Glider Network integration. Toggle independently for battery optimization.

Spot Logging

Capture observations with auto-filled timestamp, GPS coordinates, altitude, and distance. Attach photos and notes. Sort and filter by date, type, altitude, or military status.

Photo System

Capture directly or import from gallery with automatic EXIF extraction. Rate your photos, track your best shot for each aircraft type, and optionally back up to the cloud.

Gear Profiles

Save your camera body and lens combinations as reusable gear profiles. Track which setup you used for each spot and see detailed equipment usage analytics.
