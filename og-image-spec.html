OG Image Specification for SpotterHQ

This page documents what the og-image.png should contain. The PNG needs to be created manually or with a design tool.

Requirements

  • Dimensions: 1200 x 630 pixels (standard Open Graph size)
  • Format: PNG (required by most social platforms)
  • Filename: og-image.png (placed in site root)
  • URL: https://spotterhq.app/og-image.png

Design Specification

  • Background: Dark navy (#0a0f1e) with subtle radar motif
  • Primary text: "SpotterHQ" in large, bold white text with cyan-to-indigo gradient
  • Tagline: "The Ultimate Military Aviation Spotting App" below the title
  • Accent elements: Radar rings, sweep line, and blip dots using brand cyan (#38bdf8)
  • Optional: Small aircraft silhouette or radar icon
  • Brand colors: Cyan (#38bdf8), Indigo (#818cf8), Amber (#fbbf24), Background (#0a0f1e)

Meta Tag (already added to index.html)

<meta property="og:image" content="https://spotterhq.app/og-image.png">
